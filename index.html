Resonance Re-patterning

£127

This is a system I love and am so enthusiastic about offering. I discovered Resonance Repatterning many years ago and it literally changed my life. I was a single mum of three and wasn’t sure how I was going to manage. Resonance Repatterning opened doors for me and once I stepped through lots of things started to happen. I trained in Resonance Repatterning and even became the UK Co-ordinator setting up seminars for others to learn this amazing process.

Since then I’ve used it successfully with many hundreds of clients. Science has now proven that everything is energy or vibrating frequencies down to our tiniest cell. So even each thought, feeling and the words we speak have a particular resonance. When our frequencies are in tune our energy is positive and we quite simply attract what we want in our life, however, when our frequencies are out of tune we can feel depleted, tired, unmotivated and life feels stressful and challenging. We frequently feel stuck.

You already have within you all you need to attract what you want. Vibration is an energetic life force with a frequency that fluctuates. You are a collection of energy and you function on frequency from the minutest cell within your body to the wavelength of your thoughts and beliefs. Your desires all have a frequency. You control your vibration, and as you alter your vibration you alter what you attract. When your vibration matches the frequency of the situation or dream life you want, it comes into alignment and then it manifests. Efforting creates a different vibration that leads to struggle so you end up struggling to reach your goal instead of aligning with it. Even if life is going smoothly, there are times when something that we would love to manifest just isn't happening. This is because we simply aren't 'resonating' with it.

Through Resonance Repatterning you shift your frequency intentionally to create your best possible outcome. It’s a system that uses techniques from Kinesiology (muscle checking) that makes it possible for you to maintain your natural state of balance and achieve your best no matter what the circumstances of your life now. Some people have described it as miraculous. It can seem like that, but it is purely a way of tapping into your system to uncover your unconscious impulses and imprints so you can choose a different way of being.

You are literally only one thought and one action away from what you desire in your life so make it happen now. Sessions are in person in Devon, via Skype or Zoom or by phone. Distance is no object. Contact me today.
